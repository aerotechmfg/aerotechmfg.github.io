What we do

Aero-Tech Precision has served its clients in the Greater Toronto Area for more than 20 years. Specializing in custom machining, we are a facility that produces quality work for commercial clients and custom machine builders. We also serve numerous other clients in need of our precision machining services.

Your Requests

Whether you have an idea for a store fixture to display your retail items, or innovative electronic components, we have the equipment and expertise to custom create your design to your precise specifications.

From CNC turning to general and custom machining, a small run or a large one, we produce high-quality pieces accurately, efficiently and in the timeliest manner.

PROTOTYPING

Talk to us to make your design a reality. We can assist you in designing and prototyping your project.

PRODUCTION MACHINING

Our shop can produce small to medium-scale CNC production runs.

MAINTENANCE & REPAIR

We can replace custom parts, modify existing pieces, or machine into tolerance.
