What We Do

Aero-Tech Precision Machining has served its clients in the Greater Toronto Area for more than 20 years. Specializing in custom machining, we are a facility that produces quality work for commercial clients and custom machine builders. We also serve numerous other clients in need of our precision machining services.

Your Requests

We work with each customer to ensure we meet the specifications of your request.

Whether you have an idea for a store fixture to display your retail items, or innovative electronic components, we have the equipment and expertise to custom create your design to your precise specifications.

From CNC turning to general and custom machining, a small run or a large one, we produce high-quality pieces accurately, efficiently and in the timeliest manner.
